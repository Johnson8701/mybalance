How it works

4 steps, once

Set it up in 2 minutes. Then your balance is always on your home screen.

Install the APK
Download and install directly on your Android phone. No Play Store needed.
Add Home Widget
Long press home screen → Widgets → My Balance → drag to screen.
Check in GPay
Open GPay or PhonePe, check your balance with UPI PIN.
Tap + to Update
Enter the balance on the widget. It stays visible all day. Done.
Features

Built for daily use

Everything a UPI user needs to track their balance — nothing else.

Home Screen Widget
Balance visible on home screen at all times. No opening apps.
Privacy Lock
Balance hidden by default. Tap eye to unlock. Auto-locks in 60s.
Quick Add / Deduct
Green + and Red − pill buttons. Update balance without opening app.
Transaction History
Every update recorded with timestamp. Green credit, red debit.
Indian Number Format
₹ 1,00,000 not ₹ 100,000. Proper Indian formatting throughout.
Last Updated Chip
"Just now", "5m ago", "2h ago" — always know how fresh the data is.
